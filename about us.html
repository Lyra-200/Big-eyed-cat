Theme

Welcome to our shopping paradise! Doraemon's pockets will provide you with unlimited shopping options, from makeup, clothing to food, to meet your different needs, while emphasizing quality and discounts.

Our theme is Doraemon's Pockets, aiming to provide consumers with the products they dream of, with a wide range of products.

For the design of the logo, use rounded fonts to reflect the cute feeling of Doraemon. The logo reflects the two important elements of Doraemon's bell and pocket.

Password policy and Registration method

Our registration includes username, email address, password, confirm password, and reset button. The password requires a combination of uppercase and lowercase letters, numbers, and symbols, with a minimum length of 8 characters. The login form contains a username and password.

When the user's input does not meet the specifications, an error prompt will appear, and the error field will turn red; When the input is correct, it will turn black. When the user successfully registers, a pop-up window will appear, indicating that the user has successfully registered, and then jump to the login form; After successful login, the user will be redirected to the homepage.

Embellishments

Our page is based on the blue color of Doraemon, interspersed with various elements from Doraemon, including arrow design and page background design. Provide consumers with a joyful and relaxed atmosphere, and enhance their happiness in online shopping.

On our homepage, you will find the latest products and product categories. Through an intuitive interface, you can easily browse various categories, view popular products, and discover the latest trends.

Each product page provides detailed descriptions, clear images, and prices. You can confidently browse products, understand their functions, specifications, and materials, and make wise shopping decisions.

User Manual

On the homepage, you can browse various product categories, including cosmetics, clothing, food, etc. On the product page, you can view detailed information about the product, such as size, color, food taste, etc.

In order to purchase goods, you need to register an account first. When registering an account, please ensure accurate personal information is provided. If you already have an account, please log in using the login page. At all, you can click on the "Add to Cart" button.
